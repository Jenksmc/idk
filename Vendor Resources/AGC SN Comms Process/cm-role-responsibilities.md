## Overview

This page documents the role, as well as specific responsibilities, of an Azure CXP Air Gapped Clouds (AGC AzComms) operator - also known externally as the Communications Manager (CM). 

The AGC AzComms team is one of two (2) teams responsible for the creation and dissemination of Service Notification communications for Microsoft Azure. The AGC AzComms team is engaged when a planned change is scheduled to occur within the classified US government Clouds (USNat and USSec). The AGC AzComms team manages the communications that keep government customers informed about the coming change and other relevant information. 

## Role Responsibilities

Below is a breakdown of the operator/CM role and their specific responsibilities when tasked with working on an SN. SN CMs will: 

- Work with the Service PM to ensure the customer is fully informed of all relevant SN information in an accurate, and timely manner. This includes answers to the questions: 
    - Who is impacted (which Clouds/Regions, Subscription IDs, email addresses)?
    - What is impacted (which Services)? 

    - When will the change start/occur (day and time)? 

    - What change is occurring (update/maint)? 

    - Why the change is occurring (benefit to the customer)? 

    - What should the customer do (if anything)?

- Revise and publish AGC AzComms Outage communications through the use of various LS and HS tools.  

- Meet all communication service level agreement (SLA) requirements for SNs: 

    - **Acknowledge (ACK)** the SN request: <span style="color:red">**2 days**</span> from IcM creation
    - **Publish the SN**: <span style="color:red">**7 days**</span> from IcM creation 
    - **SN Retirement Cadence** accuracy: <span style="color:red">**95%**</span>  

- Continuously monitor the [SN and Publish IcMs (Sev 3, 4)](https://teams.microsoft.com/l/channel/19%3Af5333433a4b94a149009bfd95438a73b%40thread.tacv2/SN%20and%20Publish%20IcMs%20(Sev%203%2C%204)?groupId=297fb1a7-1622-4824-8fe4-f461fb04b98b&tenantId=72f988bf-86f1-41af-91ab-2d7cd011db47) Teams chat and [IcM Board](https://portal.microsofticm.com/imp/v3/incidents/search/advanced). 

- Be the first to acknowledge an SN request and engage with Service Team PMs (ST PM) when new SNs are requested.

- Follow CSM-directed external communications strategy and decisions.

- Be pro-active in asking the ST for all information needed to complete the SN communications. 

- Proofread and confirm the correctness of all communications prior to sharing with FTEs.
    - Utilize available tools, such as MS Word read-aloud, spelling and grammar checking, Copilot, etc. 

- Update all IcM tickets and assigned ADO card fields with accurate information as the SN status changes.
