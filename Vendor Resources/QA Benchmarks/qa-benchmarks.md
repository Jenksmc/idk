## Quality Assurance Reviews 

The quality of every message sent by the AzComms team is reviewed within several days of a Service Notification ADO card being marked as resolved. As part of that QA review, all the messages sent for every SN are checked by AFS leadership specifically for: 

- Spelling and grammar mistakes 
- Selection of ACM attributes with each communication (Region, Service, Event Type, Comm Stage) 
- Correct use of message templates 
- Approved LS messages match the messages sent on the HS 
- Removal of any internal (not customer-facing) information 
- Working HS hyperlinks (or commercial cloud hyperlink annotation)

Operators should always keep these specific areas of concern in mind.
